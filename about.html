My Journey

Hello, I'm Remya

My journey with mehendi began as a childhood fascination — watching my grandmother's hands transform into living canvases of intricate beauty. What started as playful doodles on paper evolved into a deep, abiding passion for this ancient art form.

Over the years, I've dedicated myself to mastering both traditional Indian patterns and contemporary styles. Every design I create is a conversation between heritage and innovation — honoring the timeless traditions of mehendi while bringing a fresh, modern perspective.

For me, mehendi is more than decoration — it's storytelling through art. Each curve, each pattern, each dot carries meaning and emotion. When I work with a bride or a client, I don't just apply mehendi; I help them wear a piece of art that tells their unique story.
