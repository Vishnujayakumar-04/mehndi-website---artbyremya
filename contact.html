Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

A full bridal mehendi session typically takes 4–6 hours, depending on the complexity and coverage of the design. I recommend scheduling the session a day before the wedding for the best stain results. We'll make sure you're comfortable throughout with a relaxing setup.
With proper aftercare, the stain typically lasts 1–3 weeks. The darkest stain appears within 48 hours of removing the paste. Factors like skin type, body temperature, and aftercare affect the stain's darkness and longevity. I provide detailed aftercare instructions with every session.
Yes! I love traveling for destination weddings across India and internationally. Travel charges vary based on the location and duration of stay. Contact me with your destination details and I'll provide a custom quote including travel and accommodation.
For bridal mehendi, I recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance, especially during the wedding season (October–February). For other events, 2–4 weeks advance notice is usually sufficient. Early booking ensures availability for your preferred date.
I use 100% natural, organic henna made from fresh henna leaves. My mehendi paste contains only natural ingredients — henna powder, eucalyptus oil, lemon juice, and sugar. It is completely safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin. I never use chemical-based or "black henna" products.
Yes! I offer trial sessions for bridal clients. This is a great way to test the stain, finalize design preferences, and discuss the overall look. Trial sessions are available as an add-on to the bridal package. Contact me to schedule one.
A booking deposit is required to secure your date. Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations within 7–14 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable. Date changes are accommodated based on availability at no extra charge.
