How It Works

Our Process

From first conversation to final reveal — a seamless, premium experience.

01

Consultation

We discuss your event, style preferences, and vision. Share reference images or let me suggest designs that match your aesthetic. This can be done in person or virtually.

02

Design Selection

Based on our consultation, I create a customized design plan. You'll review and approve the concept before your session, ensuring every detail is exactly as you envision.

03

Application

On your special day, I arrive with premium natural mehendi and apply your design with precision and care. Relaxing music, comfortable setup — a truly pampering experience.

04

Final Reveal

After the paste is removed, watch your design develop into a rich, deep stain over 24–48 hours. I provide detailed aftercare instructions for the best results.
